FAQ

how to cancel booking?

If you need to cancel a booking, please contact our agent directly. They will assist you with the cancellation process for your reservation.

How do I contact the property owner?

If you need to get in touch with the property owner, you can find their contact details in your agreement or on our platform. Log in to your account and navigate to the 'Contact' or 'Owner Information' section for the property you are interested in.

where can I pay the rent?

To pay your rent, please contact the property owner directly. They will provide you with the necessary details and instructions for making rent payments.

How can I inquire about a property?

If you're interested in a property, you can easily inquire about it by clicking on the property listing. Look for the 'Contact Agent' or 'Inquire' button, and fill out the provided form. The property owner or agent will receive your inquiry, and you can communicate further to get more details or schedule a viewing.

Why can't I find my listed property?

If you're unable to find your listed property, ensure that it has been successfully submitted and approved. Listings may take some time to appear on the platform. If the issue persists, contact our support team for assistance in resolving the matter.

Why is my property not getting visibility?

To enhance the visibility of your property, make sure it has clear and attractive photos, a detailed description, and is priced competitively. Consider reaching out to our support team for personalized tips on optimizing your listing to attract more potential buyers.

Is parking available?

Some properties offer parking spaces, while others might have nearby parking options. Check the listing details for specifics.
If it is not shown on the information, you can communicate with owner or agent.

What's the duration of the lease?

Leases typically run for 6 or 12 months, but we also offer short-term rentals or flexible lease durations.
